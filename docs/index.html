About Me

I have been interested in technology since I was a kid. It started out with playing games on our old CRT monitor computer which eventually lead to IT classes in high school. I learned the physical components well enough but that wasn't what drew me to technology. I decided to try out the software side of things, so I went to college for a semester and took a couple of intro courses in computer science. They were good for the basics but I needed something else, college just wasnt doing it for me. So after leaving I realized I had to teach myself, and that's what I've been doing for the past year and a half.

I have tried quite a few different online courses on a number of websites and am using everything that I have been learning and making it my own. I am super excited to finally be doing something that I can call 100% my own and show it off. It feels like my journey has already lasted forever, though I know I am only getting started.
